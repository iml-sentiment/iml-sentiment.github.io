Instructions

Please read carefully the following instructions. Use the arrows and on the left/right to change pages. You will be able to start the task when you reach the fifth page.

In this HIT, you will help us collect a set of words useful for machines to analyze the sentiment of movie reviews. There are 50 tasks awaiting, each provides one word for you to determine if it is relevant for understanding whether movie reviews are positive or negative. The task number is shown on the top.

Take the task on "film" for example, you will need to determine if you want to consider ("Include") this word or not ("Ignore") when analyzing the sentiments of movie reviews. You are given sample review fragments containing the word "film" for reference. "film" are all bolded and put in the center.

As you tackle the task, the computer will estimate with scores how effective including/ignoring the word is for it to judge the sentiments of reviews similar to the examples provided. It might help you with your decision- the higher the score, the better the computer can make the call. The score lies in the range of 0-100.

We display the scores right after the options you can take (Include/Ignore), with the higher scored one highlighted with an orange background Ignore, 92.751, and the other one with blue Include, 34.903. Please note the estimate could be inaccurate in many cases.

You should submit your decision with on one of the the two submit buttons. Submit your decision to move to the next task.
